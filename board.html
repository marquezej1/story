The Board Meeting

In the early 1960's, Ford was struggling to sell new vehicles to younger Americans. Henry Ford II decided to enter into the 24 Hours of LeMans, where cars race for 24 hours straight.

By winning the 24 Hours of LeMans, Ford would be able to sell more of the new Mustang.

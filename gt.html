The Legacy

Tragically, Miles was killed later in 1966 in an accident driving a new Ford GT40, called the J Car.

Ford won the next three Le Mans.

In 2019,Ford Vs. Ferrari was released, based on the story of the 1966 Le Mans victory.

Ferrari would not see another Le Mans victory until 2023.
